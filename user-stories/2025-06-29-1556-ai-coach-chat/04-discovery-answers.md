# Discovery Answers - AI Coach Chat Implementation

## Question 1: Plateau Detection Trigger Points
**Answer**: Smart default approach
- **Proactive for major plateaus**: 3+ weeks or month-long stagnation triggers automatic diagnostic conversation
- **Reactive for minor stalls**: Shorter plateaus wait for user inquiry
- **Reasoning**: Balances helpful intervention with avoiding over-notification

## Question 2: AI Personality Access Strategy  
**Answer**: Tiered access model based on subscription plans
- **Free Plan**: Limited access to simple, cost-efficient AI coach
- **Premium Plan**: Full access to multiple AI personalities (Dr. Lift, Coach Rico, etc.)
- **Monetization Strategy**: Premium AI personalities become key differentiator and upgrade driver
- **Marketing Hook**: "Unlock different AI coaching personalities in Premium"

## Strategic Implications

### Plateau Detection Implementation:
- **3-week threshold**: "I noticed your [exercise] hasn't improved in 3 weeks..."
- **1-month threshold**: "Your [exercise] has been stuck at [weight] for a month. Let's diagnose this together."
- **Context-aware timing**: Consider workout frequency (2x/week = longer detection window)

### Pricing Tier Strategy:
- **Free Tier**: Basic AI with limited personality, simple plateau detection
- **Premium Tier**: Multiple AI personalities, advanced analysis, personalized questioning approaches
- **Value Proposition**: "Choose your coaching style - science-based precision or motivational gym bro energy"
