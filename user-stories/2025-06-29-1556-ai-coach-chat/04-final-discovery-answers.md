- **Proactive small tips** - AI can offer general insights without user prompts

## Final Implementation Strategy

### Free Tier: "AI Assistant"
- **Premade Questions**: "Why am I not progressing?", "How often should I train?", etc.
- **Limited Chats**: 5-10 total conversations per account (not monthly reset)
- **General Tips**: AI can spontaneously offer workout insights
- **No Plateau Detection**: Premium feature only
- **Upgrade CTA**: "Unlock Dr. Lift personality and unlimited coaching in Premium"

### Premium Tier: "AI Coach Dr. Lift"
- **Full Personality**: Jeff Nippard/Jeremy Ethier inspired coaching style
- **Plateau Detection**: Automatic analysis after 3+ weeks stagnation
- **Injury-Aware**: Integrates onboarding injury data into recommendations
- **Unlimited Conversations**: No chat limits
- **Advanced Analysis**: Deep workout pattern recognition

### Technical Requirements
- **Onboarding Gate**: Must complete "getting started" (including injury questions) to unlock AI
- **Data Integration**: AI accesses workout history, injury profile, and progress metrics
- **Personality Consistency**: Dr. Lift maintains evidence-based but accessible tone
- **Form Limitations**: Minimal form advice due to visual limitations

### Dr. Lift Personality Guidelines
- **Language Style**: "Based on research from Jeff Nippard, your plateau suggests..."
- **Evidence References**: Cites popular science-based lifters, not academic papers
- **Approachable Tone**: Scientific but conversational, not overly technical
- **Form Approach**: General movement patterns, not specific form corrections
- **Focus Areas**: Programming, plateau breaking, exercise selection, recovery

This creates a clear value proposition: free users get a taste of AI coaching, premium users get the full Dr. Lift experience with injury-aware, plateau-detecting intelligence.
