# Discovery Questions & Answers

## Question 1: Coach Focus & Scope
**Question**: Do you envision the AI coach primarily as a workout companion that helps during exercise sessions (real-time guidance, form tips, motivation), or as a broader lifestyle coach that discusses goals, challenges, and provides support outside of workouts too?

**Answer**: Both - comprehensive coaching with workout analysis, plateau detection, injury awareness, and multiple AI personalities (science-based vs gym bro personas)

## Key Requirements Identified:
- **Workout Analysis**: Access to workout data to identify plateaus and patterns
- **Plateau Detection**: AI can analyze performance trends and ask diagnostic questions
- **Injury Integration**: Will connect to injury data from "getting started" onboarding
- **Multiple AI Personas**: Different coaching styles (science-based "nerd" vs "gym bro")
- **Comprehensive Advice**: Both in-gym and lifestyle guidance
