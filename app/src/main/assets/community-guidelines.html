Liftrix Community Guidelines

Last Updated: December 26, 2024

Effective Date: December 26, 2024

Welcome to Liftrix! Our mission is to create a supportive, inclusive fitness community where everyone can track their progress, share achievements, and motivate each other. These guidelines help ensure our community remains a positive space for all members.

1. Core Values

Liftrix is built on these principles:

  • Respect: Treat all community members with kindness and consideration
  • Honesty: Share authentic fitness journeys and accurate information
  • Support: Encourage others and celebrate their achievements
  • Safety: Prioritize health, well-being, and responsible fitness practices
  • Inclusivity: Welcome people of all fitness levels, backgrounds, and abilities

2. What We Encourage

✅ Positive Contributions

  • Share your workout achievements and personal records (PRs)
  • Post progress photos that show your fitness journey
  • Offer constructive advice and encouragement to others
  • Ask questions and seek guidance from the community
  • Celebrate gym buddies' accomplishments
  • Share workout routines, templates, and training tips
  • Provide respectful feedback when requested
  • Report content that violates these guidelines

3. Prohibited Content & Behavior

❌ Strictly Prohibited

Harassment & Bullying

  • Targeted harassment, intimidation, or threats toward any user
  • Body shaming, fat shaming, or appearance-based mockery
  • Unwanted sexual advances or inappropriate messages
  • Doxxing (sharing personal information without consent)
  • Stalking or persistent unwanted contact

Harmful Content

  • Promotion of eating disorders or unhealthy weight loss practices
  • Encouragement of dangerous exercise techniques that risk injury
  • Promotion of performance-enhancing drugs (PEDs) or illegal substances
  • Medical advice or diagnoses (users should consult healthcare professionals)
  • Self-harm or suicide-related content

Spam & Misinformation

  • Unsolicited advertisements or promotional content
  • Pyramid schemes, MLM, or "get fit quick" scams
  • Deliberate spread of false health or fitness information
  • Repeated posting of identical or similar content
  • Misleading "before/after" photos or fabricated results

Inappropriate Content

  • Sexually explicit or pornographic material
  • Graphic violence or gore
  • Hate speech, racism, sexism, or discrimination
  • Profanity or offensive language targeted at others
  • Content promoting illegal activities

Intellectual Property Violations

  • Posting copyrighted content without permission
  • Impersonating other users, trainers, or brands
  • Using trademarked names or logos without authorization

4. Content Reporting

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these guidelines:

How to Report

  1. Tap the three dots (⋮) on any post, comment, or profile
  2. Select "Report Content"
  3. Choose the most appropriate reason:
    • Spam or misleading: Unwanted promotional content
    • Inappropriate content: Violates community guidelines
    • Harassment or bullying: Targeted abuse or threats
    • False information: Misleading health/fitness advice
    • Copyright violation: Unauthorized use of content
    • Other: Specify additional details
  4. Optionally provide additional context
  5. Submit the report

What Happens Next: Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24-48 hours. Reports are confidential, and the reported user will not be notified of who submitted the report.

5. Moderation Actions

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

Warning

  • First offense for minor violations
  • User is notified via in-app message
  • Content may be removed

Temporary Suspension

  • Repeated minor violations or first-time moderate violations
  • Account access suspended for 7-30 days
  • Content removed, posting privileges revoked

Permanent Ban

  • Severe violations or repeated suspensions
  • Account permanently deactivated
  • All content removed
  • Device may be blocked from creating new accounts

Immediate Bans: Harassment, threats, sharing explicit content, or promoting harmful practices may result in immediate permanent ban without warning.

6. User Privacy & Blocking

You control your Liftrix experience:

Privacy Settings

  • Profile Visibility: Choose Public, Followers Only, or Private
  • Workout Sharing: Control who sees your workout posts
  • Discovery Settings: Hide your profile from suggestions

Blocking Users

  • Block any user from your profile or their content
  • Blocked users cannot view your profile, posts, or interact with you
  • You will not see blocked users' content in your feed
  • Blocking is private - the other user is not notified

To block a user: Profile → ⋮ → Block User

7. Fitness Safety Guidelines

Important: Liftrix is a fitness tracking tool, not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult healthcare professionals before starting new exercise programs or making significant fitness changes.

Safe Sharing Practices

  • Disclaimer your advice: "This worked for me, but consult a professional"
  • Avoid medical claims: Don't diagnose injuries or prescribe treatments
  • Promote proper form: Share technique tips responsibly
  • Respect individual differences: What works for you may not work for others
  • Progressive overload responsibly: Don't encourage dangerous lifting practices

8. Content Ownership & Licensing

Your Content

  • You retain ownership of all content you post (workouts, photos, comments)
  • By posting, you grant Liftrix a license to display your content to other users based on your privacy settings
  • You can delete your content at any time via Settings → Account → Delete Data

Others' Content

  • Do not repost others' photos, workout routines, or content without permission
  • Give credit when sharing templates or routines from other users
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property rights

9. AI Chatbot Guidelines

Liftrix includes an AI-powered fitness coaching chatbot:

  • AI advice is general guidance, not personalized medical advice
  • Do not share sensitive medical information with the AI
  • Verify AI suggestions with qualified trainers or healthcare professionals
  • Report inappropriate AI responses via Settings → Help & Feedback

10. Appeals Process

If you believe a moderation action was made in error:

  1. Email support@liftrix.app with subject "Moderation Appeal"
  2. Include your username and the date of the action
  3. Explain why you believe the decision was incorrect
  4. Our team will review within 5-7 business days

11. Changes to Guidelines

We may update these Community Guidelines periodically to reflect:

  • Community feedback and evolving norms
  • New features or functionality
  • Legal or regulatory requirements

Material changes will be announced via in-app notification. Continued use of Liftrix after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated guidelines.

12. Contact & Support

Questions about these guidelines or moderation decisions?

  • Email: support@liftrix.app
  • In-App: Settings → Help & Feedback
  • Privacy Policy: View Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

We're committed to maintaining a safe, supportive fitness community. By following these guidelines, you help create an environment where everyone can achieve their fitness goals without fear of harassment, misinformation, or inappropriate content.

Thank you for being part of Liftrix!
